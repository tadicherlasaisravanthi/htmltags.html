*Endhukantey Premanta* (translated as "Why is it Love?") is a 2011 Telugu romantic drama film directed by Karunakaran. The movie features Sushanth and Samantha Ruth Prabhu in the lead roles. The plot revolves around the love story of Aravind (played by Sushanth) and Priya (played by Samantha). Aravind is a carefree, happy-go-lucky young man who enjoys life without much thought for the future. Priya, on the other hand, is a disciplined and idealistic woman who dreams of a perfect life. The two cross paths in college, and though initially they are from different worlds, they begin to form a deep emotional connection. As their relationship progresses, both face challenges that test their feelings and commitments. Aravind’s carefree attitude contrasts with Priya’s idealism, and the film explores the complexities of their relationship, the difficulties of balancing love with personal goals, and the emotional growth they undergo through the ups and downs. The movie is known for its light-hearted yet emotional storyline, capturing the nuances of modern-day relationships. The music, composed by Anup Rubens, also plays a significant role in conveying the romantic atmosphere of the movie. *Endhukantey Premanta* is a story about love, personal growth, and the compromises and sacrifices people make for the people they care about.

Game Changer is an upcoming 2024 Telugu-language film directed by Shankar. The movie stars Ram Charan in the lead role, with Kiara Advani also playing a prominent part. It is an action-packed thriller with elements of political drama and suspense. The plot revolves around the character of Ram Charan, who is involved in a complex situation that turns his life upside down. The film explores how he navigates through a corrupt and manipulative system. As a person who finds himself caught in a web of power struggles, betrayal, and moral dilemmas, his journey becomes one of transformation. Game Changer delves into themes of power, justice, and the idea of what it means to be a true "game changer" in a world full of deception. The movie promises intense action sequences, thrilling moments, and emotional depth as it explores how a single individual's choices can affect the larger society and change the game altogether.

"Thandel" is a 2025 Telugu-language romantic action drama directed by Chandoo Mondeti, featuring Naga Chaitanya and Sai Pallavi in the lead roles. The film is inspired by a real-life incident from 2018, where a fisherman from Srikakulam was captured by Pakistani forces in international waters. The story follows Raju (Naga Chaitanya), a fisherman deeply in love with Satya (Sai Pallavi). Despite Satya's plea for him to abandon the sea and seek other employment, Raju ventures out and accidentally drifts into Pakistani waters, leading to his arrest. Mallli Malli Idhi Rani Roju (translated as Again, this is a Queen's Day) is a 2015 Telugu romantic drama film directed by K. N. T. Sastry. The movie stars Sumanth and Nithya Menen in the lead roles. The film explores themes of love, relationships, and the passage of time. The story centers around the lives of two individuals, Gautham (played by Sumanth) and Rani (played by Nithya Menen). Gautham is a young man who becomes infatuated with Rani, a woman who is a traditional, strong, and independent character. The film alternates between two timelines: one where Gautham is a young man in his early 20s, and the other where he is a more mature man, looking back at his life and the decisions he made regarding Rani.

Majili is a 2019 Telugu-language romantic drama film directed by Shiva Nirvana. The movie stars Naga Chaitanya and Samantha Ruth Prabhu in the lead roles, with a strong supporting cast including Rao Ramesh and Divyansha Kaushik. The story revolves around the life of Poorna (Naga Chaitanya), a talented young cricketer who is deeply in love with his childhood sweetheart Sravani (Samantha Ruth Prabhu). Despite their strong bond, Poorna’s career and personal choices lead to misunderstandings and complications in their relationship. Poorna, initially driven by passion and ambition, loses his way after facing failures and disappointments in life.
