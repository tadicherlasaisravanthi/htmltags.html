A mighty sea warrior takes a violent stand against the criminal deeds of his village.Set in the coastal regions of Andhra Pradesh and Tamil Nadu, the narrative follows Devara (N. T. Rama Rao Jr.), a chieftain of a coastal village, who becomes embroiled in a conflict with Bhaira (Saif Ali Khan), a rival chieftain involved in arms smuggling through the Red Sea. The story delves into themes of honor, betrayal, and the complexities of leadership within a close-knit community
